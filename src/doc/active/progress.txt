2026-04-15 — Rate-step calibration fix + gimbal model
----------------------------------------
Done:
- Diagnosed rate-step calibration failure: 0x07 GimbalSpeed needs continuous
  streaming (single publish = transient pulse / dropped packet on A8 mini).
  Fixed `phase_rate_step_response` to stream at 50 Hz for the whole step.
- Found sign mismatch between rate command and rate state: rate_callback
  didn't apply direction multipliers (state_rate did), and 0x07 yaw polarity
  on A8 mini is opposite to 0x0D attitude rate. Added `yaw_rate_cmd_sign`
  (default -1.0) and `pitch_rate_cmd_sign` (default +1.0) parameters and
  applied direction multipliers symmetrically with state_rate.
- Robustness fixes in calibration: default --rate-duration 1.0→3.0 s (reach
  steady state), inter-step brake wait 0.2→0.7 s (kill residual motion),
  latency threshold 5→2 deg/s (less acceleration-phase contamination),
  _fit_rate_step_metrics rejects traces starting past the 10% threshold.
- Analysis script: split rate_step_trajectories by axis × sign of u,
  overlay w_ss + first-order fit per trace; new rate_step_metrics plot
  (rise, latency, K_eff, w_peak/w_ss vs |u|); rate_k_curve fits +u and -u
  branches separately with deadband markers; main() now treats angle-step
  and rate-step phases as independent optional sections.
- Clean calibration run 1776262353: yaw K=73.42 deg/s per u, τ=0.101 s;
  pitch K=73.56 deg/s per u, τ=0.094 s. Linear and symmetric across
  |u|∈[0.1, 1.0], no measurable deadband, no overshoot.
- Added `gimbal_controller/gimbal_model.py`: pure-Python (no ROS) first-order
  rate-loop model with yaw/pitch defaults from the bench run. API:
  AxisParams, GimbalAxis.step(u, dt), rate_to_cmd(w), Gimbal wrapper.
  Validated against analytical first-order response.

Next:
- Run a sine-sweep phase (continuous sinusoidal u at several frequencies)
  to obtain a Bode plot — exposes higher-order dynamics, phase lag, and
  any structural resonance not visible in a step response.
- Verify `yaw_rate_cmd_sign` default of -1.0 on a second A8 mini unit (it
  may be the hardware-unit-specific 0x07 polarity).
- Consider widening pitch safety envelope or shortening --rate-duration for
  pitch so the max-|u| sweep doesn't rail (pitch envelope -18..83 is tight
  at 74 deg/s for 3 s).
- Use gimbal_model.py in sim regression tests / feed-forward control path.
